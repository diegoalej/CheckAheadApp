About the App

Check Ahead is an awesome app that lets users search for grocery stores near them to see that particular stores busiest times, so that you can plan you grocery trips to avoid unwanted lines and over-crowding. Once you figure out how much you enojy the app you can regiter to save your favorite store locations and add more stores to see other busy times during the day.
