About the App

Check Ahead is a handy app that lets users search for grocery stores near them to see that particular stores' busiest times, so that you can plan ahead for grocery trips and avoid unwanted lines and over-crowding. You do not need to register to use the app, in order to save your favorite store locations, add more stores, and leave comments you will have to register.
